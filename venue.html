Conference Venue

Hotel Fort Canning
11 Canning Walk Singapore 178881 (map)

Considered as an iconic heritage hotel in Singapore, the Hotel Fort Canning building was built in 1926 and is the result of the restoration of a former British Military Administration Building.

Directions by Train

Alight at Dhoby Ghaut MRT, look for Exit B (Penang Road) and take a 8 mins walk to Hotel Fort Canning around Park Mall.

1. Walk towards Penang Road
2. Cross the road and turn left
3. Walk up the stairs
4. Go towards the upslope
5. Pass the carpark entry
6. Walk up the stairs to the top

By Car, Where to Park?

The hotel has 120 limited lots and complimentary parking coupons are provided for parking at the hotel. If the hotel carpark is full, there is public carpark C (coupon based, S$1.00 per half an hour).

Public Carpark C, hotel is accessible from the stairs on the left
