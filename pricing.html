Why Attend

Meet and talk to authors and creators

Many of the invited speakers are authors and creators of ideas that influenced how we work today. Here is a chance to talk to the authors, ask them the background of the ideas and what have they learned since. Remember to ask for their autograph!

Connect with the top minds

This conference usually attracts people who are serious in learning to improve their software/product development practices. Feel the strong support with like-minded people and grow your wisdom from learning from the best.

Expand your perspectives for better project outcomes

Many of our speakers are forerunners of new ways to communicate with stakeholders and deal with technical challenges for better project outcomes. The diversity of the topics brings a more holistic picture of product/software development and increases the chance of you being able to apply new ideas to your current challenges.

Renew your energy and get inspired

Product/Software development is a highly demanding work that requires great adaptation in the company and also frequent changes in requirements and technology. It could be draining to some and lose the sense of purpose. Renew your energy and bring back the enthusiasm to your colleagues.

Attend a world-class conference

You wanted but couldn’t attend the notable Agile Conference in the U.S. where they have world-class speakers. We are the closest you can get in Singapore.

Satisfy your company's commitment to learning

Most companies have the budget to support staff’s learning. Take advantage of this exclusive opportunity to learn from world-class speakers and professionals best in their field.
