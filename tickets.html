Workshop subsidy
for Singaporeans/PR

Thanks to e2i (Employment and Employability Institute), all Singaporeans/PR can enjoy a subsidy of S$80 per workshop seat.

Please provide the following details:

  • Full name in NRIC
  • NRIC
  • PR/Singaporean
  • Designation
  • Workshop(s) to attend
  • Email address (Optional. By filling in your email, you give your consent to e2i to send you future event-related information)
* Not applicable to people working in Singapore government.
