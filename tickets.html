Why Attend?

Here are 6 reasons why you should come to Agile Singapore 2016 Conference

1) Speak directly to authors and creators

Many of the invited speakers are authors and creators of ideas that influenced how we work today. Reach out to the speakers and get answers to those burning questions in your head. Remember to ask for their autograph!

2) Connect with the top minds

This conference usually attracts people who are serious in learning to improve their software/product development practices. Feel the strong support with like-minded people and grow your wisdom from learning from the best.

3) Improve project outcomes

Discover new ways to achieve your project outcomes - Our speakers are influencers within the industry who brings new perspectives to communicate with your stakeholders and deal with technical challenges. The diversity of the topics brings a more holistic picture of product/software development and increases the chance of you being able to apply new ideas to your current challenges.

4) Inspire your mind

Product/Software development is a highly demanding work that requires great adaptation in the company and also frequent changes in requirements and technology. Revive your energy and bring back the enthusiasm to your team! Product/Software development can be highly demanding and draining, some even lose their sense of purpose.

5) Attend a world-class conference

You wanted but couldn't attend the notable Agile Conference in the U.S. where they have world-class speakers. We are the closest you can get in Singapore without flying to another city.
